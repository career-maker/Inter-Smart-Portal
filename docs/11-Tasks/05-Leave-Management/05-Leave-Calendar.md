# Leave Management

## Module 5

# Leave Calendar

Project

Intersmart Employee Portal

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

Implement a unified calendar system for:

- Company Holidays
- Employee Leave
- Team Leave
- Work From Home (WFH)
- Half-Day Leave
- Half-Day WFH

The calendar shall synchronize automatically with approved leave requests.

---

# Calendar Types

## Company Calendar

Managed by Super Admin.

Displays

- Public Holidays
- Company Holidays
- Events
- Optional Company Activities

---

## Employee Calendar

Displays

- Approved Leave
- Pending Leave
- WFH
- Half-Day Leave
- Half-Day WFH

---

## Team Calendar

Displays

- Team Leave
- Team WFH
- Team Availability

Visible only to:

- Team Lead
- HR
- Super Admin
