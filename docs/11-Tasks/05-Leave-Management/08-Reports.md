## Module 8

# Reports & Analytics

Project

Intersmart Employee Portal

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This module provides reporting and analytics for Leave and Work From Home (WFH).

Reports shall be available based on user permissions.

Employees shall only see their own reports.

Team Leads shall see reports for their own team.

HR and Super Admin shall have access to all reports.

Reports shall support:

- Dashboard Widgets
- Charts
- Tables
- PDF Export
- Excel Export
- CSV Export
- Print
