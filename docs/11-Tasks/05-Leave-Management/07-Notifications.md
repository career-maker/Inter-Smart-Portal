## Module 7

# Notifications

Project

Intersmart Employee Portal

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This module manages all notifications related to Leave and Work From Home (WFH).

Notifications shall be delivered through:

- Email
- In-App Notification Center
- Dashboard Notification Badge

The notification system shall support future expansion to SMS, Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Push Notifications.

---

# Notification Types

## Employee

- Leave Submitted
- Leave Approved
- Leave Rejected
- WFH Submitted
- WFH Approved
- WFH Rejected
- Leave Cancelled
- WFH Cancelled

---

## Team Lead

- New Leave Request
- New WFH Request
- Leave Cancellation
- Reminder Pending Approval

---

## HR / Super Admin

- Team Lead Leave Request
- Escalated Leave Request
- Failed Email Alert
- Leave Balance Adjustment
- Approval Delegation Activated
