# Leave Management

## Module 6

# Work From Home (WFH)

Project

Intersmart Employee Portal

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This module manages Work From Home (WFH) requests.

Although WFH is requested through the Leave & WFH interface, it shall be treated as a work status rather than a leave type.

WFH requests require approval and shall not deduct any leave balance.

---

# Supported Request Types

- Full Day WFH
- Half Day WFH (Morning)
- Half Day WFH (Afternoon)

---

# Business Rules

- WFH requires Team Lead approval.
- If the requester is a Team Lead, approval is required from HR/Super Admin.
- WFH shall not reduce Casual Leave balance.
- WFH shall not reduce Sick Leave balance.
- WFH shall appear in:
  - Dashboard
  - View The Hall
  - Team Dashboard
  - Leave Calendar
  - Reports
