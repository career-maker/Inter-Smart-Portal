# Email Workflow Specification

# Intersmart Employee Portal

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines every email generated by the Employee Portal.

All automated emails shall follow the same branding and formatting.

Emails shall be responsive and mobile-friendly.

All emails shall contain:

- Intersmart Logo
- Employee Name
- Subject
- Email Body
- Action Buttons (where applicable)
- Footer
- Disclaimer

---

# Mail Configuration

Supported Providers

- SMTP
- Microsoft 365
- Google Workspace
- Amazon SES (Future)

Laravel Queue shall be used for sending emails.

Email failures shall be logged and retried automatically.
